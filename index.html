VERMAX

Vermax was a dragon in House of the Dragon. Vermax was ridden by Prince Jacaerys Velaryon during the Targaryen civil war, the Dance of the Dragons. Vermax was a strong and fierce dragon with dark scales. This dragon played a significant role in the conflict, showcasing its power and loyalty to Prince Jacaerys. Vermax's involvement in key events during the war added depth to the storyline and highlighted its importance in the dragon lore of House of the Dragon.

HBO

MOONDANCER

Moondancer was a young, pale green she-dragon with pearl horns, crest, and wing bones. She was the mount of Lady Baela Targaryen, and although she was initially too small to carry Baela, she grew large enough to be ridden by her within a year. Moondancer remained at Dragonstone with Baela for most of the first two years of the Dance of the Dragons, where Baela trained extensively on her, riding her every day all over the island and even to nearby Driftmark and back.

HBO

SYRAX

Syrax was a dragon in House of the Dragon. Syrax was ridden by Princess Rhaenyra Targaryen during the Targaryen civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons. Syrax was described as a beautiful dragon with silver scales. This dragon was known for its speed and agility in battle, making it a formidable force on the battlefield. Syrax's loyalty to Rhaenyra and its participation in crucial events during the war showcased its importance in the narrative of House of the Dragon.



07
HBO

SEASMOKE

Seasmoke, also known as Seasmoke the Black Dread, was a powerful dragon in House of the Dragon. Prince Joffrey Velaryon rode Seasmoke during the tumultuous Dance of the Dragons. With his dark scales and imposing presence, Seasmoke was a formidable force in battles. His involvement in key conflicts highlighted his strength and impact on the war's outcome. Seasmoke's story adds depth and excitement to the lore of House of the Dragon.

HBO

SUNFYRE

Sunfyre was a magnificent dragon from House of the Dragon. Sunfyre, also known as Sunfyre the Golden, was ridden by King Aegon II Targaryen during the civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons. Sunfyre was a powerful and majestic dragon, known for his golden scales and impressive size. The dragon played a crucial role in the conflict, showcasing his strength and prowess in battles. Sunfyre's involvement in the war and his relationship with King Aegon II added depth to the narrative of House of the Dragon.

DREAMFYRE

Dreamfyre was a massive female dragon with pale blue skin and silver markings, one of the largest Targaryen dragons in Westeros. She was born during Aegon the Conqueror's reign and was ridden by Princess Rhaena Targaryen and later by Queen Helaena Targaryen. Dreamfyre was a breeding dragon, producing many dragon eggs throughout Rhaena's lifetime. During the Dance of the Dragons, Dreamfyre was housed in the Dragonpit in King's Landing, where she remained until the civil war broke out. MELEYS

Meleys, the Red Queen, was a fierce dragon ridden by Princess Rhaenys Targaryen during Dance of the Dragons in House of the Dragon. With her fiery red scales and formidable presence, Meleys was a force to be reckoned with on the battlefield. Her role in the conflict was pivotal, showcasing her strength and power in key battles. The story of Meleys enriches the narrative of House of the Dragon, adding depth to the history of House Targaryen.



03
HBO

VERMITHOR

Vermithor, also known as Vermithor the Bronze Fury, was a mighty dragon featured in House of the Dragon. Vermithor was ridden by King Jaehaerys I Targaryen during the Targaryen civil war known as the Dance of the Dragons. With his impressive bronze scales and formidable size, Vermithor was a force to be reckoned with in battle. His presence on the battlefield was commanding, and he played a significant role in various engagements during the conflict. Vermithor's story adds depth and excitement to the world of House of the Dragon.



02
HBO

CARAXES

Caraxes, the Blood Wyrm, was a fearsome dragon ridden by Daemon Targaryen, also known as Daemon the Rogue, during the intense conflict of the Dance of the Dragons. Their bond was truly remarkable, with Daemon harnessing Caraxes' power in battles to advance his cause and make a significant impact on the war. This legendary partnership between Daemon and Caraxes is a fascinating chapter in the history of House Targaryen, showcasing the strength and resilience of both dragon and rider. Caraxes was feared by his enemies and revered by his allies, and his legacy became a symbol of Targaryen power and dominance. His name, "Blood Wyrm", was earned due to his penchant for violence and the trail of blood and destruction he left in his wake.



01
HBO

VHAGAR

Vhagar is a colossal dragon in House of the Dragon, renowned for her immense size and strength. Her scales glimmer like gold and bronze in the sunlight, and her eyes burn with a fierce inner fire. She is the largest dragon alive during the Dance of the Dragons and is considered the most powerful in Westeros. Vhagar's riders, Laena Velaryon and later Aemond Targaryen, wield significant power and influence in the Seven Kingdoms due to their ability to command this formidable beast. Her strength, speed, and ferocity make her a formidable force in the skies, striking fear into the hearts of those who oppose her.
